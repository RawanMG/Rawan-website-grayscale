Rawan Alghofaili

I'm PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Boston working under the supervision of Professor Lap-Fai (Craig) Yu. Prior to venturing into the world of PhD life, I received an MEng. (Master's of Engineering) in Computer Science from Cornell University.

I'm interested in using data as a mean to create Computer Graphics applications. This involves blending the wonderful worlds of Cognitive Science, HCI, VR, Vision and Fabrication to produce functional data driven designs.

Research interests: Computational Design, Virtual Reality, Graphics, Vision, HCI and Cognitve Science.
